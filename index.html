Random Forest Index Futures Forecaster

Role: Developer

Status: Deployed. Refined and in use after handoff

Built a prototype classification model during internship at investment bank. After refinement, currently in use by equities trader.

Technologies: Python, VBA

Built a prototype classification model in Python using Bloomberg API data to forecast directional movement (up/down/flat) of Southeast Asian index futures based on features like volatility and volume. Initial tests showed ~80% directional accuracy on historical data, though likely subject to overfitting. Created during a summer internship on the equities desk at a bulge bracket investment bank. After I returned to university, the model was further refined and used by the trader desk-head in live decision-making.

Barter EdTech App Launch

Role: Co-founder, Product & Marketing Lead

Status: MVP Launched · Paused Post-COVID

Peer language-learning for students. Think Tinder meets tandem exchange.

Technologies: Figma

In 2021, I co-founded a peer-learning edtech startup that helped undergrads connect over language learning. Think “Tinder for language exchange,” but grounded in academic collaboration and authentic conversation. I led marketing and coordinated design direction across our small team, working closely with designers and developers to build and launch our MVP.

Visual identity was key for me. I worked closely with our designers to build a visual identity that feels welcoming and collaborative.


Meanwhile, I led our direct marketing strategy, which allowed us to quickly expand across six universitis, with notable organic adoption—including a language professor who promoted the platform to her students as a peer-built initiative.

Though our traction grew, momentum slowed when universities resumed in-person teaching and student routines normalized post-COVID. However, by that time, the MVP, visual identity, and promotional push were fully realized. Today, the project is paused—but the experience remains foundational to how I think about product, language, and user-centered design.
